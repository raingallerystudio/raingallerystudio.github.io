Mixing

A good mix brings the music to life. I offer remote mixing and mastering services for artists and bands with a focus on rock, metal, and hardcore music. My goal is to understand your creative vision and make it a reality. You record the performances and send them to me—I do the rest.

If you're looking for creative input and songwriting help in addition to mixing/mastering I'm happy to help co-write and produce your music. Use the contact form below to get in touch and we'll discuss how to make your record a reality.
